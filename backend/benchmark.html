Metric Guide

Silhouette Score
Measures how similar each student is to its own cluster vs others.
≥ 0.50 Strong   0.25–0.50 Reasonable   < 0.25 Weak
Range: −1 to +1
Davies-Bouldin Index
Average ratio of within-cluster scatter to between-cluster separation.
≤ 0.5 Excellent   0.5–1.0 Good   > 1.0 Overlapping
Range: 0 to ∞, lower is better
Calinski-Harabasz Score
Ratio of between-cluster dispersion to within-cluster dispersion.
Higher values indicate more dense, well-separated clusters.
No fixed upper bound — compare across runs.
Inertia (WCSS)
Within-cluster sum of squares — total distance of each point to its centroid.
Lower is better. Use to compare different k values or training runs.
Not comparable across datasets of different sizes.
